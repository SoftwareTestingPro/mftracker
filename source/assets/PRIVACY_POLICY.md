# Privacy Policy for mftracker

**Last Updated: May 06, 2026**

mftracker ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### No Personal Data Collection
mftracker does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. We do not require you to create an account with us.

### Anonymous Tracking
The app tracks mutual fund data that you manually add. This data is handled anonymously and is never associated with your identity by our application.

### Google Drive Integration
mftracker uses Google Drive to store your investment data. 
- **Storage**: Your data is saved in a hidden "App Data" folder on your own Google Drive.
- **Access**: We only request access to the specific files created by this app. We **do not** read, access, or modify any other files or folders in your Google Drive.
- **Privacy**: Your data stays in your Google account. We do not have access to your Google Drive credentials or any other data in your Google account.

## 2. Permissions

### Google Sign-In
Used solely to authenticate you with Google so that the app can save your data to your own Google Drive.

### Internet Access
Required to sync your data with Google Drive and fetch latest mutual fund NAVs (if applicable).

## 3. Data Security
Since your data is stored in your own Google Drive, it is protected by Google's industry-leading security infrastructure. We recommend using two-factor authentication (2FA) on your Google account for enhanced security.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
